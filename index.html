Groovypedia About us

Our Story

Groovypedia

is a European music record label that records, publishes and distribute live music sessions.
Groovypedia was founded in 2013. At the moment, we have producers from 12 different cities who produce exclusive videos for Groovypedia.

It's all About Music It's all About Music

Music Platform

Our aim is to share the best new music with all the music lovers around the world by using every opportunity online & offline. We've produced more than 500 live music sessions worldwide. We accept bands of all size and genres.
Groovypedia shares revenue from digital sales of live session recordings with the musicians, usually split 50:50.
If you'd like to contribute into this platform as a producer or as a live performer, reach us at: hi@groovypedia.com

Production

We've started producing performance videos in studios since February 2013 under the name "Groovypedia Studio Sessions and we would love to see you by our side!

Finance
